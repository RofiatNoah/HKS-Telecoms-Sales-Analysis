## 📌 Project Overview
I developed a dynamic **Business Intelligence (BI) solution** for **HKS Telecoms** to transform raw sales records into actionable strategic insights. By leveraging **Advanced Excel**, I built a centralized system to track **₦9.84M in revenue** and analyze profitability across various dimensions including time, location, and product categories.

This project demonstrates the ability to turn static numbers into a "business story" that helps stakeholders move from guessing to data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🛠 Technical Implementation
* **Data Transformation (ETL):** Leveraged **Power Query** to automate the cleaning, shaping, and structuring of raw transaction data.
* **Data Modeling:** Built a robust model to calculate high-impact KPIs, including an aggregate **36.69% Profit Margin**.
* **Interactive Visualization:** Engineered a user-centric dashboard featuring **Slicers and Timeline Filters** for real-time data exploration.
* **Statistical Analysis:** Grouped data to identify significant performance variances between **Weekday and Weekend** operations.

---

## 📈 Key Business Insights
* **Profitability:** Successfully tracked **₦3.25M in Total Profit**, identifying **Wearables (₦3.7M)** as the leading revenue category.
* **Operational Peak:** Analysis revealed that **Weekdays** account for **64% of total revenue (₦6.3M)**, pinpointing the most active business periods.
* **Geographical Hubs:** **Yaba (₦2,111,500)** and **Ajah (₦1,998,000)** emerged as the primary revenue drivers for the business.
* **Product Velocity:** Essential accessories like **Power Banks** and **USB Cables** were identified as the highest-volume items (350+ units combined).

---

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations
* **Inventory Management:** Implement an "Always-In-Stock" policy for Power Banks and USB cables to prevent revenue leakage.
* **Regional Marketing:** Allocate 60% of marketing budgets toward **Weekday** promotions specifically targeting the **Yaba** and **Ajah** demographics.
* **Product Bundling:** Launch a "Smart Tech Bundle" (pairing Audio accessories with Wearables) to increase the average transaction value.
